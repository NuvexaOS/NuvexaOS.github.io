Why It Feels So Fast

NuvexaOS runs drivers & services as isolated micro-servers. A rogue Wi-Fi driver crash? Your desktop keeps humming.

  • Pre-emptive zero-copy channels slash latency.
  • MsgQ batching coalesces interrupts for snappier UI.
  • zRAM 2 compresses idle pages → 40 % RAM savings.

Security Without Pop-ups

The Nux Policy Engine vets app manifests once, then enforces silently via eBPF. Less “Allow?” nagging, more doing.

  • Per-app VPN routing
  • Automatic SSH/PGP key rotation
  • Kernel-level anti-cheat compliance
